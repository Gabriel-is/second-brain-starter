# Today I Learned

Knowledge log. When you learn something new during a session — a concept, a tool, a pattern — Claude captures it here. Over time, this becomes a searchable record of everything you've picked up.

## How It Works
- Each entry has a short title and a brief explanation
- Tagged by topic area for searchability
- Claude adds entries when you encounter new concepts
- You can ask "what do I know about X?" and Claude searches this

## Getting Started
As you work with Claude, it will naturally encounter moments where you learn something. It logs them here. You can also explicitly say "add that to my TIL" for anything worth remembering.
